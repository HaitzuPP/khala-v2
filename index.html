The Thesis

AI's real
constraint is
not compute.

The funds that get this right will be positioned in the correct layer of the stack before the rest of the market understands what the stack is. We track the protocols, the metrics, and the capital flows that make that distinction legible before consensus arrives.

01
The constraint is coordination, not compute.
Frontier AI progress is no longer blocked by raw processing power. It is blocked by the inability to align distributed global talent around the hardest problems. Without bureaucracy, without borders, without a central orchestrator.
02
Tokenisation is the first mechanism capable of solving this at scale.
For the first time in history, cryptographic incentive layers can coordinate millions of contributors across jurisdictions, in real time, around a single technical objective. Without asking anyone's permission.
03
The winning protocols
have not yet been priced.
Bittensor's top subnets generate measurable recurring revenue. Leading compute networks bill enterprise clients today. AI data protocols have signed Fortune 500 partners. None of this is reflected in token valuations. The market is still applying 2021 speculation frameworks to infrastructure that is already in production. That dislocation is the trade.
