The Crypto Wedge
01
The labs are winning by design, not by merit.
OpenAI, Google DeepMind, and Anthropic have raised over $300B collectively. All three have closed weights. None have public governance. The most transformative technology in human history is being built behind closed doors.
OpenAI: closed weights · $157B raised · no public board accountability Google DeepMind: integrated into ad monopoly · no external governance
02
Crypto is the only counter that cannot be reversed.
Regulation can be lobbied away. Open-source pledges can be revoked. Safety commitments are voluntary. Cryptographic incentive layers cannot be captured — permissionless by design, enforced by protocol, not by a CEO.
Token emissions: no gatekeeper can block a contributor anywhere on earth Deregistration: quality enforced by protocol, not by institutional authority
03
The equitable AI stack is being built right now, pre-consensus.
Distributed training, open coordination networks, censorship-resistant AI infrastructure. None of it priced in. The window between “building” and “won” is where Khala operates.
Bittensor: 128+ subnet network · permissionless · no VC lock-in Gensyn: distributed training · active testnet · open contributor model
04
These teams need to be found before the window closes.
Once the labs control the model layer, the distribution layer, and the regulatory conversation — there is no wedge left. The teams building the alternative need capital and attention now, not after the consolidation is complete.
Frontier capability doubling every 12–18 months · concentration accelerates OpenAI staff now advising government AI policy · regulatory capture in motion
